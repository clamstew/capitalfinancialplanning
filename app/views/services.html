Services Overview

Capital Financial Planning offers financial planning services which vary in scope from a consultation to a comprehensive financial plan and from a review of investments to active portfolio management. You select the service appropriate to your situation.

Initial Consultation

There is no fee for the initial meeting in which we explore which of our services are suitable for you.

Compensation

Investment management fees are based on a percentage of assets under management. Life insurance or annuities are provided through highly rated companies on a commission basis. Other services may be provided on an hourly basis if preferred.
